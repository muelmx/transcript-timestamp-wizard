Transcript Timestamp Wizard

A simple tool to transform transcripts generated by Adobe Premiere® to a common format (todo).

How To

  1. Choose one or more files.
  2. Click "transform".
  3. The results will be downloaded automatically.

The conversion happens locally on your browser. No data is leaving your device.
